1)What is a kickball foul?,When a player intentionally uses their foot to contact the ball
2)What is an illegal screen?,When a player sets a pin screen and begins to move 
3)What is a technical foul?,A foul given to players displaying poor behaviour e.g swearing/violence
4)What is a shooting foul?,Act of fouling player while shooting OR restricting landing area of shooter
5)What is a flagrant foul?,When a player commits a very physical foul intentionally
